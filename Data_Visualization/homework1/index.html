2. Visualization Critique
i. Who is the intended audience?
    I think the audience is common people. because there are a lot of explanation for professional noun such as platelet, cholesterol particle and Hemoglobin protein.
ii. What design principles does the visualization utilize?
    The main idea of data visualization is to "tell a story". There are to goals in data visualization. One is explain data to solve problem and the other is explore data for better understanding. Obviously, this one is try to explore data for us to understand how our bloodstream work and contain.
    The principle I think used in this data visualization is followed,
Connectedness: the author use a male body to illustrate the what is in our bloodstream. From heart to oxygen atom, which is from big to small. All this things is wrapped by the male body. Which makes us know they are in one system.
Similarity: author draw the things the same sharp and color. For example, red blood cell, author make them to be all red and like a donut. So we know that other “red donut” also is red blood cell.
Closure: author draw a blood vessel from heart to all cell. Which indicate us that in the blood vessel there are read blood cell, platelet, cholesterol particle, hemoglobin protein, heme group and oxygen atom. The author do not need to say anything. We can tell by ourselves.
iii. Do you like or dislike this visualization? Why?
    I like this visualization. Because even I am not a biology student. I can understand what happens in our bloodstream.
iv. What can you imagine from the visualization
    Like I said in question 2. With closure law. I can imagine. In my body, there are a lot of things in my blood. They all have different function.
v. What do you think about the color used in the work
    Most of color used is fine with me. Because I know blood is red. And for platelet I think it should be that color. So every color the author used is follow my common sense.
vi. Can you suggest any improvements?
    I think if the author can make some animation will be better. To show the relation between all the cell.
